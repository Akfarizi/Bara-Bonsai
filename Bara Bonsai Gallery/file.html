Profile & History

Bara Bonsai Gallery was founded by Bara, a bonsai enthusiast who has nurtured his passion since his teenage years. Starting from a single small tree in a pot, his hobby grew into a private gallery filled with classical bonsai styles. Bara’s mission is to share the beauty of bonsai with a wider audience while preserving traditional techniques and aesthetics of this living art.
